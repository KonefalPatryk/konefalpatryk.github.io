Motion Capture Test (Personal Pandemic Project 2020)

I wanted to learn how games work and played around with game files with various programs and learned how to connect a Kinect v1 & Kinect v2 to the computer and how to use it for motion capture and photogrammetry, and then used a character model from the game BattleForge (2009) now called Skylords Reborn, to then use it to learn about rigging and weight painting and combining it all together for a short video.

The recording demonstrates the use of a single Kinect v2 sensor, resulting in noticeable jitter in the animation due to its limitation of tracking from only one direction.

Upon reflection I have learnt how to better optimize an animation through changing the recording environment and making sure it's set under the a ppropriate conditions for the best tracking motion tracking quality. ​Key things I've Learnt: Rigging Weight Painting Motion Capture Photogrammetry ​
